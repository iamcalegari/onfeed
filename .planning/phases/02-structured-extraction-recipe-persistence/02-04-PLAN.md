<objective>
Build the pure confidence/review gate that turns the per-field grounding produced by the extractor (Plan 02) into a single `confidenceScore` plus a `reviewRequired` boolean. This is the structural enforcement of EXT-05: it is impossible for a low-confidence or unsafe extraction to skip review. The function is deterministic and has zero dependencies, so it gets full unit coverage. It is consumed by the pipeline integration (Plan 05), which persists its output onto the Recipe/ImportJob and sets `status: ready_for_review` accordingly.

Purpose: EXT-02 requires each field to carry a confidence signal and an aggregate score; EXT-05 requires low-confidence extractions to be routed to mandatory review rather than published silently. This plan owns the honest computation and the override rules that a self-graded LLM score alone cannot be trusted for.
Output: a pure `computeConfidence` + its test, ready for Plan 05 to wire into the pipeline.
</objective>
