<objective>
Widen the existing likes -> promotion machinery to promote a confirmed imported recipe from private to public when it is both trusted (confidence bar) and liked (+5 third-party likes), without ever letting likes alone promote a low-confidence recipe and without ever flipping its `source` away from `"imported"`.

Purpose: SOC-04 requires +5 likes AND the confidence bar to promote an imported recipe to the public catalog (D-06), reusing the `env.variants.promoteThreshold` like bar (D-07); D-05 says the promotion is a visibility flip (private -> public) that KEEPS `source:"imported"` (so grounding + credits survive); D-08 excludes the owner's own like from the count; D-09/SOC-05 means credit retention falls out for free because `source` never changes. This plan adds the `promoteImportToPublic` mutation and widens `maybePromote` to apply the full D-06 gate with owner self-like exclusion.
Output: `promoteImportToPublic(recipeId)` in recipe.repository.ts, a widened `maybePromote` in like.repository.ts, a repository test suite proving the gate + self-like exclusion + credit retention, and an updated likes module README.
</objective>
